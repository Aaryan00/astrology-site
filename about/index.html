Executive Biography

Vedic Wisdom Meets Humanitarian Leadership

Shri Shri Amit Agarwal is internationally recognised as one of the world's most trusted, most accurate and highly recommended celebrity Astrologers, Vastu Consultants, Palmists and Numerologists. Based in Kanpur, India, his accurate guidance, global reputation and profound spiritual insights have earned him a worldwide following.

Beyond the esoteric sciences, he is a celebrated Motivational Speaker, Life Coach and Writer, dedicated to transforming lives. As a dynamic entrepreneur and institutional builder, he serves as the Managing Director of Home Food Products and is a distinguished pillar of Rotary International.

Widely appreciated and honoured with prestigious national titles, his blend of Vedic wisdom and humanitarian leadership makes him a truly iconic figure — deeply trusted and admired worldwide.
